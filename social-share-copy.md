## Facebook / community group version

Deka Lake property owners should not have to learn the rules through enforcement.

Deka Lake is a rural, seasonal, recreational lake community. Many of us pay taxes, maintain property, support local businesses, and use our land in practical ways that reflect the area's history and character.

The CRD needs to work with the community before enforcement escalates.

Please send a message asking for a dedicated Deka Lake / Interlakes public meeting, plain-language rules, fair transition options, and risk-based enforcement focused on genuine safety and environmental concerns.
